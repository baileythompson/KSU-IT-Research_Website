KSU Center for Applied Research in Information Technology

Welcome to the Kennesaw State University Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) website. CARIT is research center that provides insight into important topics in information technology through a variety of time- and peer-tested methods. CARIT's purpose is to better interact with local businesses and communities through the use of discoveries in information technology and its uses.

Be sure to click through our site to learn what we're up to, how you can contact our esteemed staff with your questions and more.
