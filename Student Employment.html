KSU Center for Applied Research in Information Technology

Want to work in research with some of the top minds in Kennesaw State University's Information Technology Program? Consider appyling to be one of four student assistants at KSU CARIT this summer!

You will assist in the creation of the research process, gather answers from test subjects, compile them and report your findings. You will have the opportunity to witness every step of the professional information technology research process on a variety of projects that are already in their various stages. You will also learn what the life of an information technology researcher is like, and perhaps be inspired to become one yourself!
